# Sales_Performance_Dashboard_tableau


## Objective
Create an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze sales, profit, customer segments, and product performance.

## Dataset
Global Superstore Dataset

## Tools Used
- Tableau Public

## Dashboard Components
- KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Orders)
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Sales Distribution by Segment
- Top 10 Products by Sales

## Key Insights
- Technology is the highest-selling category with sales of 4.74M.
- North region generates the highest profit.
- Consumer segment contributes 51.48% of total sales.
- Corporate segment accounts for 30.25% of total sales.
- Apple Smart Phone is the top-selling product.

## Conclusion
Technology products drive the majority of sales, while the Consumer segment contributes the largest share of revenue. The North region is the most profitable region, indicating strong market performance.
